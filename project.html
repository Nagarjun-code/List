OUR CAMPUS

CLASS ROOM

There are sufficient no of Classrooms for regular teaching and these are
well ventilated and they offer right atmosphere for learning.
The white interactive boards and bulletin boards placed at proper places
make learning easier and interesting. The strength of each class
is kept at a manageable level and comfortable furniture adds
to the ambiance of the classrooms.

LIBRARY

The library is a quintessential part of an educational institution; in fact, it is its very soul.
It is the place students can sit and learn in a tranquil environment,
at their own pace. The onus of gathering, categorizing, maintaining, updating,
and disseminating information through various books and digital resources, is of course,
on the institution. The Library can accommodate 30 people in the reading area,
and it has nearly 30,000 books, on the topic of contemporary management.

SPORTS

Physical Education is an integral part of the total education system. Sports develop
discipline, the spirit of sportsmanship and inculcates the qualities of team spirit
& leadership among the students. In order to make our students
the champions, we bring them with the belief that they are the best and our cheerful
champions participated in different sports competitions and won medals,
certificates, trophies and brought a lot of laurels to our school.

TRANSPORTS

Keeping in mind the convenience of the students as well as the staff members we have
fleet of well managed GPS enabled buses that are well maintained,
equipped with CCTV's, and manned by trained drivers, conductors and maids.
The buses are regularly maintained, and are fitted with speed governors
to meet the traffic regulations imposed by the transport authorities of the city.
